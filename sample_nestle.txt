Website Title: Nestlé — Good Food, Good Life

Nestlé is a global food and beverage company headquartered in Vevey, Switzerland. 
Founded in 1866, Nestlé operates in over 180 countries and has more than 2000 brands ranging from global icons to local favorites. 
Its major product categories include dairy products, infant nutrition, coffee, bottled water, confectionery, pet care, and healthcare nutrition.

Nestlé’s mission is to unlock the power of food to enhance quality of life for everyone, today and for generations to come. 
The company focuses on nutrition, health, and wellness while driving sustainability through responsible sourcing, water stewardship, and reducing greenhouse gas emissions. 

Popular brands under Nestlé include Nescafé, KitKat, Maggi, Nespresso, Milo, and Purina. 
Nestlé also invests heavily in research and innovation through its global R&D network to create healthier and more sustainable food solutions. 

Nestlé’s purpose-driven approach centers on creating shared value for shareholders, communities, and the planet.
